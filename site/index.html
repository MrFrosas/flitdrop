Frequently asked

Is it really free?

Yes, on your own Wi-Fi.

Do I need to install anything on the phone?

No. You scan a QR code once and the phone is paired.

Is it really private?

Everything is end to end encrypted and stays on your local network.

Which devices does it work between?

iPhone, Android, Samsung on one side, Windows, Mac, Linux on the other.

Do both devices need to be on the same Wi-Fi?

Yes. Flitdrop goes straight from one device to the other on your local network, so they share the same Wi-Fi. Nothing travels over the internet.

Is there a size or number limit?

No. Since the transfer stays on your network, there is no imposed cap. The only limit is the free space on the receiving device.

Which computers can I install it on?

Windows, macOS and Linux. The phone needs nothing at all, just its browser.

Are my photos compressed?

Never. Photos and files arrive at their original quality, exactly as they were.
